Our Mission Statement

To make creative projects get the light they deserve. No matter what, creators always control how the work comes together. When backers contribute either by funding or extending a creative hand and help spread the word, they too become part of these independent works, a part of our community.

Meet our team

Started off during the pandemic in 2020 with 5 College kids we are an independent company working off of bangalore, india trying to give people the platform we would like to be a part of and help the community by giving life to their ideas
