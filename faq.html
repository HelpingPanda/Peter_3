When does my crowdfunding campaign go live?

The crowdfunding will go live immediately after your documents have been verified. Keep an eye on your inbox for any mail from us, we will also intimidate you before your campaign goes live.


How do you review my crowdfunding campaign?

We verify your credentials (PAN number, Mobile OTP verification and social graph) submitted along with fundraiser details, supporting documents (if any) and publicly available information (website, Facebook, Twitter accounts). Post that, we also do a call if we have further questions.


Can donors get tax-exemption for this?

INR donations made by Indian taxpayers can avail tax benefit if you contribute to a campaign towards an NGO which has an 80G certificate. Look out for the Tax Benefits tag on the campaign page. Receipts are usually issued once the campaign ends.


Can I raise money for anything?

Currently due to the Corona Pandemic, we are targeted mainly towards NGOs and charities. We want to help as much as we can to those in immediate need.

What if I don't reach my goal?

We give you the option to cash out how much you have collected after the end date of your campaign.


How many campaigns can I run ??

Right now, each user can have only one active campaign. After the end of a campaign, the user can set up another one.
